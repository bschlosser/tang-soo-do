Chil Sang Sam Ro

  1. Fall into double low palm strike (I think that's what it's called)
  2. Rise up, separate hands, step out, breathe, press
  3. Step back double knife hand strike/block (like Pyong Ahn O dan, but hands open)
  4. Turn, double inside/outside block, side kick, retreating Pyong Ahn Sa Dan motion
  5. Turn, repeat
  6. RIGHT FOOT IN, RIGHT FOOT OUT
  7. Step right, high block/uppercut
  8. "Propeller" motion
  9. Settle, breathe, push
  10. LEFT FOOT IN, RIGHT FOOT OUT (crossing axis of form)
  11. Step left, high block/uppercut
  12. "Propeller" motion
  13. Settle, breathe, push
  14. Right foot steps over, K blocks (Pyong Ahn Ee Dan)
  15. Step center, check block
  16. Step, double low cross block
  17. Step, cover face, double high block, break
  18. Step, double kidney strike
  19. Step, double punch KEOP
  20. Lowest possible block
  21. (Sa Dan run) High knife hand block/strike, front kick, skip-in backfist KEOP
  22. Retreat middle knife hand block
  23. Horse stance palm, step horse stance palm, step front stance double spear hand KEOP
  24. Drop fingers, right foot in/out double ridge hand, knife hand strike
  25. Turn, repeat
  26. Left foot in/out, high block
  27. Beat the drum
  28. LEFT FOOT IN, RIGHT FOOT OUT, high block KEOP
  29. Beat the drum
